Welcome To Busidex!

Busi-What? Busidex brings you to them and them to you. It's like a backpack for your business - always available and always current. Using our easy-to-use web and mobile platform, you can add and update and distribute your business card online and have it work for you like never before.

So what does Busidex do for you?

  • Everyone on Busidex that has your card will always see your current contact information as soon as you update it.
  • Let's you share your card with anyone you want with one click
  • Helps you manage your business without getting in your business.
  • Gives you near real-time insight into how effectively you're getting your information out there.
