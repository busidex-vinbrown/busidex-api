The New Busidex Website
      on the desk or on the go...

It's been a long time coming, but it was well worth the wait. The new website is finally here and will be available to the public on 7/11/2015. We hope you'll find that there's a lot to like! We have been listening carefully to our customers and getting a good understanding of how people want to use the Busidex website.

Here's a quick overview of the new stuff you'll find in the new website:

  • A fresh new professional look
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Easier card updates
  • One-Click filters in My Busidex
  • Thumbnail or detail card views
  • FAQ page

Our New Look

Thanks to Darren Marinelli and Artinium for the great new site design. In listening to customer feedback, we found that many of you aren't sitting down at your computers surfing the web, but rather you are using your mobile devices to do your web browsing. Now you can use the Busidex website on your phone just as easily as on your laptop.

Adding or Updating Your Card

If you have joined Busidex in the past two months, then this will be no surprise. But for those who joined earlier, I invite you to go to start.busidex.com and update your card. This new layout should be easier to understand and will work on your phone just as easily as your home computer.

A New Way to See Your Card

Searching for cards will give you some new options now. With the new layout, you can view your search results as thumbnail images or by card details.

Organizing My Busidex

Now your My Busidex collection is easier to manage with new 'one-click' filters. On the left side of the screen you will find a list that lets you filter your cards quickly and easily. Click (or tap on your phone) on the letter to see cards who's name begins with that letter.

Any Questions?

We have added a FAQ page to answer some of the more commonly asked questions to give users quick answers and get them back to being productive. This page will be updated regularly as we get more user feedback, so if there's something you would like included on this page, just let us know.
