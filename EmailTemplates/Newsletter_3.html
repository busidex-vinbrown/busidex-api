You're Sitting on a Gold Mine!
Do you use Gmail? Have you checked your contact list lately? You probably have hundreds of contacts - some you deal with regularly, others on and off, and some you haven't reached out to in months (or longer). Busidex lets you share your card with them and attach a personalized message along with it.

To use this feature, go to My Card -> Share and click the Gmail icon. Note: if you use another email provider like Yahoo or Outlook, those will be supported soon. After clicking the icon, you may be prompted to log into your Gmail account. Keep in mind that Busidex does not store your Gmail username or password. So be assured that your account(s) are secure. If you happen to have multiple Gmail accounts, you can switch between them by clicking the 'Switch Accounts' button.

Select the contacts to whom you want to send your card, and even include a personalized message. When you're ready, you can preview your email to make sure it looks alright. Click 'Share Your Card Now' to send it to all the contacts you have selected, and you're done!
