Busidex Newsletter #2

We've been hard at work on some new features and we're rolling them out as fast as we can. Here's the latest and greatest features and tips for getting the most out of your Busidex account.

Tag! You're It!
Have you added any Tags to your card? Tags are like key words that help people find your card. For example, if you're a Realtor then you might want to add Tags like 'Real Estate' or 'homes'. You can add Tags to your card by going to "My Card" and clicking the Edit button.

Our goal is to provide as much value to our users as possible, so that those that want to (see the next bit for more about that) can get their card in front of as many people as possible.

Expanded Privacy Settings
Some Busidex users may only want to share their card with a select number of people. To allow for that, we have added some additional privacy settings where you edit your card. Go to "My Card" and click the Edit button, and there you will find that you have three options:
  • Public - Anyone can find your card, add it to their collection and share it.
  • Semi-Public - Your card can be found only by those with whom it has been shared, and anyone can share your card.
  • Private - Your card can only be found by those with whom it has been shared, and only you can share your card.
