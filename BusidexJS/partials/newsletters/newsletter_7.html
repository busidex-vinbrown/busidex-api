Busidex Newsletter - July 2014

Hope everyone is having a busy summer (and getting some vacation time in as well). I'd like to take this opportunity to give a huge thanks to our beta testers for their incredible feedback. Thanks to their comments and suggestions, we have been able to fix many usability issues and will be rolling out some great new features over the next few weeks.

There's an App for That!
The Busidex Mobile App is in the process of getting a complete rewrite. The new application will have a better user interface (UI) design and will have some additional new features that are aimed at making it dead simple for users to find and add your card to their collection.

Take a Look At Me Now!
Well, not *now*, exactly, but very soon, the Busidex website will be getting a facelift. This month we'll be working with a design partner to enhance the look and feel of our website to make it easier to navigate and give it an overall more polished and consistent look.

Featured Cards
In July we had two new featured cards: Julie LeBlanc of Keller Williams Realty and Bob Mahl of North American Power . You can add the featured card to your Busidex collection right from the home page by clicking 'Add This to Your Collection' (You need to be logged in for this).
